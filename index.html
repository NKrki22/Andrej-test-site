About me


About me l am a Serbian racing driver trying to achieve my dream of becoming a Formula I driver. I started racing karts when i was 7 years old and from 2015-2023 i have managed to Win numerous Serbian and European Championships! 2023 was my most successful year being crowned the Rotax WORLD CHAMPION! In 2024 i am competing in the Formula 4 Saudi Arabian Championship and after the first 2 rounds i am leading the championship with 6 podiums and a race win!
